Information We Access To provide its core functionality, the App may request access to the following: - **Google Account Information:** Your email address and basic profile information to facilitate the login process. - **Google Drive Access:** The App requests access to your Google Drive files to identify, play, and apply audio enhancement (volume boosting and EQ settings) to your stored audio files (e.g., MP3, WAV). ### 2. Data Sharing and Third Parties **Volume Booster : Max Sound EQ** does not sell, rent, or share any data obtained from your Google Drive with third parties. Your data remains on your device and within your Google Drive environment. ### 4. Data Retention and Deletion If you disconnect your Google account or delete the App, our access to your Google Drive is immediately revoked. Any temporary cached data on your device is cleared upon uninstallation. ### 6.
