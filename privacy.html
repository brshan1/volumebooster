Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

At Volume Booster : Max Sound EQ, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application and its integration with Google Drive.

1. Information We Access

Our app requests the drive.readonly scope to access your Google Drive. This allows you to browse, select, and stream audio and video files directly from your cloud storage into our app for sound enhancement.

2. Local Processing (No Data Collection)

We believe in absolute privacy. All audio processing, volume boosting, and equalization happen locally on your device.

  • We do not collect or store your Google Drive files on our servers.
  • We do not share your files or personal information with any third parties.
  • Your Google Drive data is used solely for the real-time playback and enhancement features within the app.

Google API Services User Data Policy (Limited Use Disclosure)

Volume Booster : Max Sound EQ's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. Security

We use industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocols to authenticate with Google. Since we do not have our own servers for data storage, your files remain securely within Google's infrastructure at all times.
